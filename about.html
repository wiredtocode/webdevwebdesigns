About Us

Web Dev Web Designs

Empowering Small Businesses with Corporate-Level Services

My name is Arminder, and I am the owner and sole developer of Web Dev Web Designs. I first got into programming during college, and even as a dropout, I have continued to teach myself while working full-time. Recently, I faced a serious injury that required me to stay at home for two months. Coincidentally, during this time, I also welcomed a niece into the family. It was then that I realized I wanted a career that would allow me to work from home and be present with my family. Determined to pursue this dream, I embarked on a journey to teach myself how to code websites from scratch. Armed with newfound skills, I reached out to small businesses, offering my assistance in creating websites that were handcrafted and tailored to their needs. I soon discovered that many small businesses faced significant challenges.

They lacked the financial resources to invest thousands of dollars upfront in a new website. Alternatively, those who could afford it often fell prey to unscrupulous developers, resulting in poorly designed websites that looked outdated and lacked modern design trends. Witnessing their frustrations, I knew I had to make a difference. That's when I decided to adapt my business model to cater to their specific needs. I introduced my innovative $0 down and RM150 a month model, making it more manageable for small businesses to handle without straining their bank accounts.

My goal is to empower small businesses with top-notch web solutions that don't break the bank. I believe that every business, regardless of size, should have access to a professional and appealing online presence. With dedication, creativity, and a deep understanding of their needs, I'm committed to helping small businesses thrive in the digital realm.
